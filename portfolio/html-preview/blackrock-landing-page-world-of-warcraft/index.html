About our project

Our team in the past, same like you, have has conquered & explored the vastness of World of Warcraft. A few years later, we again decided to re-live those emotions & moments which we have experienced during the game expansion The Burning Crusade.

But, unfortunately, we could not find a suitable place where we could re-live all the unforgettable moments & experience. Therefore, we decided to create our own server. And now, after 5 years of active development we are ready to introduce you to our project.

For the time we worked on our core of about 10 developers, we continue to actively correct any bugs which are found within the game.

Play with Progressive?

To get started, we will answer the question: "What does it mean «Progressive World?»." It is a world in which all PvE content becomes available gradually, meaning that you will not be able to visit the «Black Temple» or «Sunwell Plateau» immediately after server opening. First, you will have to defeat all the bosses of previous raids.
